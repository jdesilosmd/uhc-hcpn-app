Introduction to HCPNs

The Universal Health Care (UHC) Law in the Philippines aims to provide equitable access to quality and affordable health care for all Filipinos. A key strategy for achieving this is the establishment of Health Care Provider Networks (HCPNs).

An HCPN is an integrated and coordinated group of health care providers, from primary care to tertiary hospitals, working together to deliver a comprehensive range of health services within a defined geographical area (province or city-wide health system).
